Areas of Focus

Research Interests

Labour Economics

Employment, wages, unemployment dynamics, and labour market transitions in South Africa.

Micro-econometrics

Programme evaluation, non-parametric estimation, panel data methods, and limited dependent variable models.

Development Economics

Poverty, inequality, social grants, and economic mobility in developing contexts.

Behavioural Economics

Behavioural interventions, field experiments, and the intention–behaviour gap.

Education Economics

Returns to schooling, teacher quality, and the impact of education policy on the labour market.

Predictive Analytics

Machine learning approaches to demand forecasting, applied through Predictive Insights.
